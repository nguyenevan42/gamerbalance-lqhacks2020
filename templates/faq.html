We created GamerBalance as part of a collaborated effort for submission in LiquidHacks 2020. Our aim is to
increase the health and wellness of the gaming and eSport communities. Our team is passionate about mental
and physical well-being, so we decided to create a platform that allows players of all shapes and sizes to
encourage the community to stay healthy.

GamerBalance is a project developed to enhance the minds and bodies of gamers across the globe. Our tools make it easier
for gamers to focus on their bodies as well as enhance their playtime, through premade workouts tailored to your favorite game,
nutrition information, as well as personal training assistance.
We believe that a strong body results in a strong mental acuity. Not only do you have fun playing games, but you can also get a great
workout in to maintain your body!
